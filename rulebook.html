NATIONAL LEVEL VIRTUAL HACKATHON 2021
ABOUT THE EVENT
Innovathon 2K21 is an initiative by Innovathon-SSCE to come together with an objective of creating and implementing new ideas.

Sri Sairam College Of Engineering Bangalore CSE Department is organizing Innovathon to construct and strengthen the innovative environment.

The objective of this hackathon is to provide technical and innovative solutions to the existing local and community problems. The hackathon will help students to showcase their innovative intellectual ability to provide solutions for the real life challenges.
THEME
The theme for Innovathon 2K21 is 'Innovation for life' which intends to innovative solutions for current real life problems.
CATEGORIES
1. Game development using AR & VR
2. Agriculture Robotic Development
3. ChatBott using AI
4. Development of E-Learning Platform
